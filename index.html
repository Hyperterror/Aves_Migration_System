Bias Analysis & Fix

The original dataset had a 13.5:1 class imbalance — 93% Success, 7% Failed — producing a fake 100% accuracy. Four targeted fixes resolved this.

🛠️ The 4 Fixes Applied

  • 1

    Stricter Success Labeling

    Tightened home-return threshold from 100 km → 60 km. Forces borderline trips to be labeled "Failed" giving more real negative examples.

  • 2

    SMOTE Oversampling

    Synthesizes new "Failed" samples by interpolating between existing ones. Applied only on training folds to prevent data leakage.

  • 3

    Per-Bird Sample Weights

    GREG_5 contributed 52% of all events. Weight = 1/events_per_bird so each bird contributes equally regardless of tracking duration.

  • 4

    Stratified K-Fold CV

    Replaced single 80/20 split with 3-fold stratified CV. Every test fold is guaranteed to contain both Success and Failed samples.
